Introduction

Welcome to Wellington Bushwalks. In this website, you will find six region pages with selected bushwalks and information about them. It also has resources for other websites about bushwalks if you want to continue researching them. We've got you covered from the forests in Wainuiomata to the beaches along the Kapiti Coast.

This website is not a comprehensive list of every bushwalk in the Wellington region, but it’s a good place to start.
